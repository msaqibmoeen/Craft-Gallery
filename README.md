# Craft-Gallery
Craft Gallery is a modern, responsive art gallery website designed to showcase and sell curated artworks through a clean, premium user interface. The site includes a complete navigation structure (Home, About Us, Collection, Services, Contact) and a search feature to help visitors quickly find paintings. The hero section introduces the gallery’s identity and value proposition, guiding users to explore the collection with clear call-to-action buttons.

The “About Us” section explains the purpose of the gallery—promoting art appreciation and cultural involvement—while highlighting community-focused activities such as exhibitions, workshops, and educational events. To build credibility, the website displays key statistics (total artworks, featured artists, and years of experience), helping visitors trust the gallery and understand its presence in the art space.

The main “Collection / Sales” section is organized with category filters (e.g., Landscape, Nature, Urban), making browsing simple and intuitive. Each artwork card includes the title, category, price (PKR), a short description, and an “Order Now” button to support a smooth purchase journey. The layout emphasizes readability and visual hierarchy so users can compare artworks easily.

In addition to showcasing paintings, Craft Gallery offers professional services such as custom drawing, designing, commissioned painting, and art management (collection handling, organizing, conserving, and exhibition planning). A testimonials section adds social proof through client feedback. The Contact section provides location, phone, email, gallery hours, and a booking/message form, making it easy for visitors to schedule a visit or request support. Overall, Craft Gallery demonstrates strong front-end structuring, UI/UX planning, and business-focused presentation for an art-based brand.
